Tuition Timings

Morning Batch : 6:00 AM onwards
Noon Batch : 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM
Afternoon Batch : 4:00–4:30 PM – 6:00–6:30 PM
Evening Batch : 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Note: Morning and Noon batches are only available during exam period.

What about tests?

Weekly tests are conducted every Sunday and results are updated to parents via WhatsApp.

What about Masti and Fun?

At TTC we believe that a student learns best when they are not forced or pressurized. Teachers teach in a fun way that keeps students attentive and interested.

What about strictness?

Teachers at TTC are equally strict when necessary to maintain discipline and learning focus.

What about notes?

Notes, Worksheets, Practice Papers and Tests are provided by TTC. Special notes requested by students are also arranged.

What about fees structure?

For fee details please contact us directly or visit the centre.

No. of Teachers?

4 teachers (including 1 teacher specifically appointed for asking questions orally).
