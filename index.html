❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Which classes do you teach? ▾
We teach students from Class 1st to Class 10th across all major boards.
Which subjects are covered? ▾
We cover Maths, English, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Social Studies (History, Civics, Geography), Economics, Hindi, Marathi, G.K., M.S., and Computer.
☀️ Tell me about Summer Classes? ▾
TTC offers two summer programmes: Foundation Classes for Class 1–6 (reading, handwriting, basic maths, grammar) and Advanced Classes for Class 7–10 (answer writing, maths fundamentals, science basics, grammar). Classes starting from 5th May, 2026. Fees range from ₹500 to ₹800 per course depending on the subject. We also offer Combo Discounts if you enroll in multiple subjects!
⏰ What are the tuition timings? ▾
🌅 Morning Batch 6:00 AM Onwards 📌 Exam period only
☀️ Noon Batch 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM 📌 Exam period only
🌤️ Afternoon Batch 4:00–4:30 PM to 6:00–6:30 PM 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM

⚠️ Morning & Noon batches are available during exam period only.

How can I enroll my child? ▾
You can fill out the contact form below, call us, or visit our tuition center in Wardha directly to enroll.
📝 What about tests? ▾
Weekly tests are conducted every Sunday. Results are updated to parents via WhatsApp so you're always in the loop about your child's progress.
🎉 What about Masti and Fun? ▾
At TTC, we believe a student learns and understands better when they are not forced or pressurized. Our teachers teach in a fun, engaging way that keeps students attentive and genuinely interested in learning.
⚖️ What about strictness? ▾
Teachers at TTC are equally strict when necessary. We maintain the perfect balance between a fun learning environment and discipline to ensure the best outcomes for every student.
📚 What about notes & study materials? ▾
TTC provides Notes, Worksheets, Practice Papers, and Tests — all included. Special notes specifically demanded by students are also provided on request.
💰 What is the fees structure? ▾
For regular fee details, please contact us using the form below or visit us at the centre directly. For Summer Classes, fees range from ₹500–₹800 per course depending on the subject.
👩‍🏫 How many teachers are there? ▾
TTC has 4 dedicated teachers, including 1 teacher specifically appointed for asking questions orally to strengthen students' verbal understanding and confidence.
📱 What is the TTC Parent Dashboard? ▾
The TTC Parent Dashboard is a free digital tool exclusively for enrolled TTC students. It lets parents track their child's daily homework, test scores, syllabus completion, weak areas, and personalised teacher messages — all in one place, anytime on their phone. No app download needed!
🔐 Who can access the Parent Dashboard? ▾
The Parent Dashboard is only available to families with an enrolled child at TTC Wardha. Once enrolled, parents receive their access link. It is not publicly available to ensure student data privacy.
📊 What does the Parent Dashboard track? ▾
The dashboard tracks: today's homework with subject-wise details, test reports with scores and teacher notes, chapter-by-chapter syllabus progress, live performance graphs, subject strength analysis, and a personalised message from your child's teacher. It also shows a Homework Tracker and Syllabus Tracker so parents always know exactly where their child stands.
🎯 What are the TTC Quizzes? ▾
TTC Quizzes are custom-built interactive MCQ quizzes for subjects like English, Maths, Science, Computer, and more. Each question includes instant feedback and a detailed explanation so students understand the concept — not just the correct answer. Quizzes are chapter-specific and aligned to what's being taught in class.
📤 Can quiz scores be shared with parents? ▾
Yes! After completing a quiz, students can share their score report directly to parents via WhatsApp in one tap. The report includes the score, emoji feedback, performance rating, and a summary — making it easy for parents to stay updated without needing to ask.
💸 Is the Dashboard or Quiz free? ▾
Absolutely 100% free for all enrolled TTC students. There are no hidden charges, no subscriptions, and no app purchases. The Parent Dashboard and all subject quizzes are included as part of enrolling your child at TTC Wardha.
